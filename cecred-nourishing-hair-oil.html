Details
Grounded in our signature African oil blend, Nourishing Hair Oil is infused with 13 luscious oils—including sea buckthorn, baobab, moringa, black seed, castor, sunflower seed, watermelon seed, olive, sweet almond, flaxseed, golden jojoba, argan, and coconut—to reinforce your hair’s lipid barrier and seal in moisture, ensuring hair stays hydrated and nourished for longer. When absorbed into hair, it leaves hair feeling soft and flexible with a natural shine that’s not weighed down by silicone coating. Free of silicone, SLS/SLES, phthalates, and parabens.

WHO IT’S FOR
Dry, dull, medium to thick hair seeking manageability and shine.

SIGNATURE FRAGRANCE
Rich and complex, Temple Oud has notes of oud, Australian sandalwood, warm musk, night-blooming jasmine, Haitian vetiver, and violet leaves.

How to use
  1. Apply sparingly after styling from mid-shaft to ends to add a natural shine.
  2. Apply to towel-dried hair to add moisture and nourishment.
  3. Apply to your scalp or press into locs and braids to add moisture and shine.
  4. Mix with your conditioner for added nourishment.
